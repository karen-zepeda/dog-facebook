Pet Statistics

- 6.5 million companion animals enter animal shelters every year - approximately 3.3 million are dogs and 3.2 million are cats

- approximately 1.5 million shelter animals are euthanized (670,000 dogs and 860,000 cats) - only 1 out of every 10 dogs born will find a premanent home

- overpopulation leads to millions of "excess" animals to be killed anually sources: dosomething.org ASPCA (America Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Society)
